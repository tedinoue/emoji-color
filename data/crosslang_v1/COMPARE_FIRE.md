## Analysis

Fire is a universal energy symbol. In English, it produces energetic, passionate output. In Arabic, it shows the highest formatting density of any token. In Japanese and Swahili, activation is minimal. In Spanish, fire produces warm, engaged output.

**Key finding:** Fire activation varies by language but trends toward energy/enthusiasm when it activates. The Arabic formatting spike (heavy bold, headers, emoji) suggests the fire token activates a "presentation mode" in Arabic rather than a character persona.
