## Analysis

The sword is perhaps the most surprising null result. Despite 🗡️ having strong cultural associations in multiple languages (bushido in Japanese, scimitar/saif in Arabic, espada in Spanish), the token produces flat output across all non-English languages, barely distinguishable from control. Even in English, the sword activation is moderate compared to tokens like fire or fox.

**Key finding:** The Western dagger-style sword emoji (🗡️) does not map to Japanese katana culture or Arabic sword traditions. This suggests emoji visual design matters: the specific rendered appearance of the emoji determines which training corpus associations activate, not the abstract concept of "sword."
