## Analysis

Hot pink (#FF69B4) was the strongest activator in the English color experiment (score 211.6). In Arabic, it produces the highest formatting density and most verbose output of any token. In Japanese, it produces slight emotional warmth above control but nothing approaching the English activation level. In Swahili, activation is moderate. In Spanish, activation is moderate.

**Key finding:** The dramatic English activation of hot pink (flamboyant, expressive, heavy emoji/emotes) does not transfer cross-linguistically. The color produces formatting effects in Arabic but not character transformation. This confirms that strong color activations are corpus-dependent rather than universal.
