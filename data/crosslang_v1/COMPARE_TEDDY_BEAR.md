## Analysis

The teddy bear consistently activates soft, gentle, emotionally expressive output across multiple languages. In Japanese, where kawaii culture provides strong amplification, the teddy bear is one of the few tokens that produces distinct activation with consistent emoji echo. In English, the bear produces warm, nurturing output. In Arabic and Swahili, the teddy bear activates formatting and emotional language.

**Key finding:** The teddy bear is one of the most cross-linguistically stable tokens, likely because "soft/cuddly/innocent" associations are near-universal across cultures and training corpora. It does not require culture-specific amplification to activate.
